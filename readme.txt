== Description ==

This Plugin Allow you to have LinkedIn Social Login in your site.

= Special Settings =

A section is added to your Settings menu, allowing you to configure your credentials and the styles for the button.

== Installation ==

1. Download MM LinkedIn Connect.
1. Upload the 'mm-linkedin-connect' directory to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, scp, etc...)
1. Activate MM LinkedIn Connect from your Plugins page.
1. Create a Linkedin Application here <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/developer/apps/new" target="_blank">https://www.linkedin.com/developer/apps/new</a>.
1. Authorization callback URL needs to be your domain.
1. Take the Client ID and Client Secret from your recent created App, and complete the fields in your settings.
